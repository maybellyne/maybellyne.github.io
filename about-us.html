ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

CAFSO-WRAG is a humanitarian, not for profit, and non-governmental organization established in 1994 in Ibadan, Nigeria. Its main task is to challenge the structures and institutions that perpetuate poverty, hunger and preventable diseases, and campaign for the fulfilment of all socio-economic and human rights for sustainable human and ecological development. Our target group cuts across migrants, Diasporas, children in especially difficult circumstances, youths for self-fulfilment, women gender with a special interest in political and economic empowerment for the betterment of the society.

VISION

A society free of injustice, human rights abuse, poverty, hunger and preventable diseases for a sustainable human and environmental development.

MISSION

To campaign for the fulfilment of rights, ecological protection and human development in all ramifications.

OBJECTIVES

Towards Sustainable Ecological and Human Development

AIMS

  • To promote human rights by thinking globally but acting locally.
  • To facilitate the transition from youth to responsible adulthood.
  • To empower the community for poverty alleviation.
  • To strengthen national development through migration and Diaspora intervention.
  • To challenge injustice and promote accountability and good governance.
  • To provoke issues on peace and environmental protection
